Lasagna

Description

This classic lasagna recipe features layers of rich meat sauce, creamy ricotta, and gooey mozzarella cheese. It's a comforting Italian dish perfect for family dinners or special occasions.

With its savory flavors and satisfying texture, lasagna is a favorite for both kids and adults. Serve with a side salad and garlic bread for a complete meal.

Ingredients

  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 2 cups ricotta cheese
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 2 cups marinara sauce
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C). Cook lasagna noodles according to package instructions.
  2. In a skillet, heat olive oil and sauté garlic. Add ground beef and cook until browned. Stir in marinara sauce and simmer for 5 minutes.
  3. In a bowl, mix ricotta cheese, egg, salt, and pepper.
  4. Spread a layer of meat sauce in a baking dish. Add a layer of noodles, then ricotta mixture, then mozzarella. Repeat layers, finishing with sauce and mozzarella on top. Sprinkle with Parmesan.
  5. Bake for 30-35 minutes until cheese is bubbly and golden. Let rest for 10 minutes before serving.
