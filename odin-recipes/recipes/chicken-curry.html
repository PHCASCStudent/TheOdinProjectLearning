Chicken Curry

Description

This chicken curry is a flavorful and aromatic dish made with tender chicken pieces simmered in a rich, spiced sauce. It's a comforting meal that's perfect with rice or naan.

The blend of spices and creamy sauce makes this curry a favorite for weeknight dinners or special occasions.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb chicken breast, cut into pieces
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon ginger, grated
  • 2 tablespoons curry powder
  • 1 can coconut milk
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish

Steps

  1. Heat oil in a large skillet. Saut√© onion, garlic, and ginger until fragrant.
  2. Add chicken pieces and cook until browned.
  3. Stir in curry powder and tomato paste, cook for 2 minutes.
  4. Add coconut milk, salt, and pepper. Simmer for 20 minutes until chicken is cooked through and sauce thickens.
  5. Garnish with fresh cilantro and serve with rice or naan.
